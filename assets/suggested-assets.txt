CHARLESTON TEA — SUGGESTED CUSTOM ASSETS
=========================================

All assets should be designed with the CofC Maroon (#6B1D1D) as the primary brand color.
Export all assets at 3x resolution for Retina displays.

─────────────────────────────────────────
APP ICON
─────────────────────────────────────────
File: app-icon.png
Size: 1024x1024px (Expo will generate all sizes)
Concept: A minimalist tea cup or a stylized "T" lettermark inside a soft rounded square.
         The background could be a warm cream or deep maroon.
         Avoid gradients — flat or very subtle shading only.
         Should read clearly at 60x60px on a home screen.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SPLASH SCREEN
─────────────────────────────────────────
File: splash.png
Size: 1242x2688px (iPhone Pro Max — scales down)
Concept: Centered wordmark "Charleston Tea" or just the icon on a clean background.
         Background: Warm off-white (#F8F7F5) or deep maroon (#6B1D1D).
         No text other than the brand name — keep it minimal.

─────────────────────────────────────────
WORDMARK / LOGO
─────────────────────────────────────────
File: logo-light.svg, logo-dark.svg
Concept: "Charleston Tea" in an elegant serif or refined sans-serif.
         Could include a small tea leaf or steam motif.
         Light version: maroon on transparent
         Dark version: white on transparent (for dark headers/modals)

─────────────────────────────────────────
TAB BAR ICONS
─────────────────────────────────────────
These should be custom SVG/PNG icons at 28x28px (export at 3x = 84x84px).
Keep strokes clean, 2px weight, no fill (outline style).

feed-icon.png         — A simple horizontal list/feed icon (three stacked lines with left dots)
identities-icon.png   — An abstract mask or two overlapping circles (representing anonymity)
settings-icon.png     — A minimal gear or sliders icon

─────────────────────────────────────────
EMPTY STATE ILLUSTRATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────
File: empty-feed.png (400x400px)
Concept: A subtle, flat illustration of an empty bulletin board or foggy campus scene.
         Muted maroon/cream tones. No text in the image — handled by code.

─────────────────────────────────────────
IDENTITY AVATAR FRAME
─────────────────────────────────────────
File: avatar-frame.svg
Concept: A decorative ring or subtle frame that goes around the colored avatar circle.
         Used in IdentityManagerScreen to show the "active" identity.
         Thin maroon ring with a subtle glow effect.

─────────────────────────────────────────
ONBOARDING BACKGROUNDS
─────────────────────────────────────────
File: welcome-bg.svg or welcome-bg.png
Concept: Very subtle College of Charleston campus texture or silhouette —
         think Randolph Hall outline, the Cistern, or the College's iconic ironwork patterns.
         Should be used as a light watermark at 5-10% opacity behind the welcome screen.
         Cream or maroon tones only.

─────────────────────────────────────────
CHANNEL ICONS (optional — nice to have)
─────────────────────────────────────────
dating-icon.png       — Minimal heart or two overlapping leaf shapes (keep tasteful)
general-icon.png      — A CofC building silhouette or simple campus landmark
lore-icon.png         — A small scroll or quill / magnifying glass icon

─────────────────────────────────────────
NOTES FOR DESIGNERS
─────────────────────────────────────────
- Primary brand color: #6B1D1D (CofC Maroon)
- Background color: #F8F7F5 (warm off-white)
- All icons should follow a consistent stroke weight (2px at 1x)
- Avoid drop shadows in icons — use them only in UI layer
- Do not use gradients in icons — flat color preferred
- All app icons must follow Apple Human Interface Guidelines for app icon masks
- Export PNG at @1x, @2x, @3x for all UI icons
- Export app icon as a single 1024x1024 PNG (Expo handles resizing)
